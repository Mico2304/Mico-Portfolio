About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary artist and developer who finds joy in bringing ideas to life through various mediums. My journey began with traditional art, where I developed a deep appreciation for photorealism—capturing the intricate details of light, shadow, and texture on paper and canvas.

As my creative horizons expanded, I discovered the world of 3D modeling and animation. Blockbench became my digital canvas, where I craft voxel characters and environments that blend artistic vision with technical precision. Each model tells a story, each animation breathes life into pixels.

Programming entered my life as a natural extension of my creative process. Writing code allows me to build interactive experiences, automate workflows, and push the boundaries of what's possible in digital art and game development. It's where art meets logic, and imagination meets implementation.

Whether I'm sketching a photorealistic portrait, rigging a 3D character, or writing elegant code, my goal remains the same: to create work that resonates, inspires, and stands the test of time.

"Every piece of art, whether traditional or digital, is an opportunity to learn, grow, and share a piece of my vision with the world."
